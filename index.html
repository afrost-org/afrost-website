About AFROST

We are a group of people who have come together to solve issues communities face while building and managing them.

Members of AFROST(Association for Promotion of Free and Open Source Technologies ) have been involved in the open source communities building for over two decades.

Guiding Principles

AFROST Activities

Be a collaboration hub for open source communities, providing strategic direction, developing financial, organizational, institutional support.

Foster open-source communities and help them grow.

Organize various events eg: meetups, hackathons, dev sprints, conferences for promotion of open source technologies.

Free Lecture or Webinars to bridge the gap between skillset of fresh graduates and the expectations of the industry with the help of various training and mentorship programmes.
