About Us

  • What we do?

    We are a group of technocrats, makers, hackers, designers involved in building & supporting the open source ecosystem around the world. We have established & nurtured self-sustaining volunteer driven communities such as PyDelhi, PyData, ILUG-D, LinuxChix India, PyChill. AFROST members have been educating students in various schools & colleges across the country.

Communities with us

PyDelhi

PyDelhi is a group of technically inclined students, professionals and teachers and entrepreneurs who want to learn something new and it is a volluteer driven community.

ILUG-D

We are linux users from Delhi NCR region. We mostly include students and professionals who uses linux. We believe in endorsing OSS/FOSS and linux use and development of the same. As of now we organise monthly meetup.

LinuxChix India

LinuxChix is a community for women who like Linux and for anyone who wants to support women in computing.

GNDG

GNDG(Greater Noida Developer Group) is a community who interact with most of the Greter Noida colleges in support for open source work and technologies around us.
